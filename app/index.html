Hey There, I'm Derrick!

I am a Front End Developer currently residing in Charlotte, NC. I majored in Criminal Law and Forensics but that doesn't stop me from chasing my true passion which is designing, coding, and web development. Criminal Law and coding may seem incredibly different but there are quite a few similarities in overall thought process between the two. Both require incredible attention to detail and the ability to think ahead to plan and organize steps. When I am coding I feel like I am planning out and "dissecting" the structure of a web app and I believe that main concept is what led me to coding while I was pursuing my degree.

Experience

I have 5 years of experience working with clients in web development but I started off designing websites in Photoshop over 10 years ago. I am currently experienced in using modern tools such as HTML5, CSS3, Javascript (including ES6), jQuery, Gulp, Node/npm, Git, Webpack, Babel and am currently embarking on my adventure with React/Redux.
